About Me

I am Md. Sabbir Ahmed, currently working as a contractual lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) of Brac University and also pursuing my MSc in CSE from the same institution. I have also graduated from the following university with a CGPA of 3.97 in Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering. During the course of my academic career, I managed to acquire 4 semesters of work experience as a Student Tutor at the Department of CSE. In both my academic and professional life, I have been constantly praised as a hard-working and efficient person by my professors and peers. My key skills include a personable, committed approach to teaching, a drive to help students improve, problem-solving, critical thinking, and learning new things regularly. I have strong leadership skills, am a team player, and always have the willingness to learn. I can get the best out of students and develop positive classroom environments. I also have achievements as a researcher in the field of Machine Learning and Deep Learning. I apply proven research, critical thinking, and multitasking skills and am very passionate about teaching.
